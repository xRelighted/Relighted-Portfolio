FAQ

Common Questions

How do payments work?

I typically request 50% upfront and 50% upon completion. This ensures commitment from both sides and allows me to dedicate proper time to your project.

What formats do you deliver?

All animations are delivered in Roblox-compatible format (.rbxm) along with the source Blender files. Video previews are included.

Can I request changes after delivery?

Yes, each package includes revision rounds. Additional revisions beyond the included amount can be negotiated at a reasonable rate.

Do you work with all rig types?

I work primarily with R6 and R15 rigs. Custom rigs may require additional setup time which we can discuss during the consultation.
